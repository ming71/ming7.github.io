Research Interest

 My primary research interests are generic object detection, object detection in remote sensing images, few-shot learning, and deep learning theory. My current research topics include:
  • Misalignment between classification and regression in object detection
  • The ambiguity in the representation of the oriented targets in the remote sensing images
  • Optimization algorithms for training neural network
  • Object detection for densely arranged targets in aerial images
