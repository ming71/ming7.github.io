Short Bio

 I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), supervised by Prof. Lingjuan Miao. My research interests are mainly in generic object detection, oriented object detection, remote sensing, and deep learning theory. Recently, I'm working on probabilistic graphical model and 3D object detection.
